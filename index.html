Product Overview

RuralHealth CLI is a lightweight tool designed for rural healthcare providers. It features automated symptom triage, teleconsultation booking, and health education resources.

Symptom Checker

Automatically assess symptoms and provide triaging advice to healthcare workers.

Teleconsultation

Enable remote consultations with healthcare professionals through SMS or email.

Offline Health Education

Provide resources for managing common diseases like malaria and cholera.
